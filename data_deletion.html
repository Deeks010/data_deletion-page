Data Deletion Instructions

If you want to delete your data that our app has collected, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to your-email@example.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. In the email, include your full name and the Facebook ID associated with the data you want to delete.
  3. We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days.
